Why Nudge

A planner that listens, then does the arranging.

Most planners ask you to tap through forms — date, time, duration, repeat. Nudge flips that around. You talk; it schedules. Three things make that feel effortless.

Voice-first planning

Tap the mic and describe your day in plain language. Nudge understands what you mean — creating, moving, shortening and clearing tasks — and your schedule updates as you speak. Prefer to type? You can do that too.

It learns your routine

The more you plan, the better it fits you. Nudge quietly picks up how long your usual tasks tend to take and the rhythms you prefer, so when you leave out a detail it fills in a sensible default — and slides things to a free slot when they’d collide.

Private by design

Your speech is transcribed right on your device — the raw audio never leaves your phone. Only the text of what you said is sent on to interpret your plan. No analytics, no ad trackers, nothing sold.

How it works

From a sentence to a scheduled day.

No setup ritual. Open the app, speak, and you’re planned.

  1. Say it out loud

    Hold the mic and talk through your day, or just one change. Natural phrasing is fine — times, durations, “tomorrow”, “after lunch”, “every weekday”.

  2. Nudge arranges it

    It reads your existing tasks and learned preferences, then creates, updates or clears items to match — resolving clashes by sliding to the next free slot.

  3. Glance and go

    Your day appears as a clean morning/afternoon/evening list. Drag to fine-tune, or undo the last change with a tap if it wasn’t quite what you meant.

Privacy isn’t a setting. It’s the design.

We built Nudge to collect as little as possible: your account, the tasks you create, and the preferences it learns to help you plan. That’s it. No advertising, no third-party trackers, and your data is never sold or shared.
