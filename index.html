Why Nudge

A planner that listens.

Most planners ask you to tap through forms — date, time, duration, repeat. Nudge flips that around. You talk; it schedules.

Voice-first planning

Tap the mic and describe your day in plain language. Nudge creates, moves, shortens, or clears tasks live as you speak — and you can always type instead.

Deep work — thesis draft 9:30 Lunch with Priya 12:30 Focus block 14:00

It learns your routine

Nudge picks up your typical durations and rhythms, fills in sensible defaults, and slides colliding items to the next free slot.

Private by design

Your speech is transcribed on your device — raw audio never leaves your phone, and only the text is sent on. No analytics, no ad trackers, nothing sold.

How it works

From a sentence to a scheduled day.

No setup ritual. Open the app, speak, and you’re planned.

  1. 01

    Say it out loud

    Hold the mic and talk through your day the way you’d tell a friend.

  2. 02

    Nudge arranges it

    It reads your existing tasks and learned preferences, then resolves any clashes by sliding items to the next free slot.

  3. 03

    Glance and go

    Your day appears as a clean morning, afternoon, and evening list. Drag to fine-tune, and undo any change with one tap.

Say it your way

Things you can say.

“Block 9–11 for thesis writing.” “Add a short walk before dinner.” “Every weekday, 7 a.m. gym.” “Move lunch to 1 and shorten it.” “Clear my evening.” “Push my run to 7 a.m.”

Built private

Privacy isn’t a setting. It’s the design.

Your voice is transcribed on your device. The raw audio never leaves your phone — only the text of what you said is sent on to plan your day.

There are no analytics or ad trackers, nothing is sold or shared, and your data is isolated to your account.
